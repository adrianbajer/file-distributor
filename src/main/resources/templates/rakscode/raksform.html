RAKS code form

How it works?

Publications are marked by a code called RAKS code.
Choose one of the codes and click "View" - you will see component files of the publication.
Choose one of the codes and click "View on map" - you will see a geographical range of the publication.
Choose one of the codes and click "Download" - you will receive an Excel file with attributes of component files.
If you click "Upload" - you may update component files' attributes. Change data in downloaded Excel file and upload it.
